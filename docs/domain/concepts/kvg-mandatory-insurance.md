# KVG - Mandatory Basic Insurance

## Overview

The **Krankenversicherungsgesetz (KVG)** is the Swiss federal law that mandates basic health insurance for all residents. It ensures universal access to healthcare while allowing competition among insurers.

> **German**: Grundversicherung, Obligatorische Krankenpflegeversicherung (OKP)
> **French**: Assurance obligatoire des soins (AOS)
> **Italian**: Assicurazione obbligatoria delle cure medico-sanitarie (AOMS)

## Insurance Obligation

### Who Must Be Insured?

Every person residing in Switzerland must obtain basic health insurance, including:

| Category | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| **Swiss citizens** | Within 3 months of birth |
| **New residents** | Within 3 months of taking residence |
| **Cross-border workers** | Special rules apply (choice of CH or EU) |
| **Asylum seekers** | Covered by canton until status determined |

## Covered Services (Leistungskatalog)

### Mandatory Benefits

All KVG insurers must cover these services identically:

| Category | Coverage |
|----------|----------|
| **Medical Treatment** | Doctor visits, specialists with referral |
| **Hospital Care** | General ward (Allgemeine Abteilung) |
| **Medications** | Listed on Spezialit√§tenliste (SL) |
| **Laboratory** | Diagnostic tests |
| **Physiotherapy** | With medical prescription |
| **Maternity** | Full coverage, no cost sharing |
| **Prevention** | Defined screening programs |
| **Emergency Abroad** | Up to 2x Swiss tariff |
| **Medical Aids** | Listed items (MiGeL) |

### What's NOT Covered

| Service | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| Dental care | Only accidents, not regular care |
| Private hospital rooms | VVG supplementary required |

| Alternative medicine | Limited; some models include more |
| Cosmetic procedures | Never covered |

## Cost Sharing

### Franchise (Annual Deductible)

The insured person pays 100% of costs until the annual franchise is reached.

**Available Franchise Levels**:

| Age Group | Options (CHF) | Default |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Children (0-18)** | 0, 100, 200, 300, 400, 600 | CHF 0 |
| **Adults (19+)** | 300, 500, 1000, 1500, 2000, 2500 | CHF 300 |

‚ö†Ô∏è **Rule**: Franchise can only be changed on **January 1st** each year. Deadline to notify insurer: **November 30**.

### Selbstbehalt (Co-Payment)

After the franchise is reached:
- Insured pays **10%** of remaining costs
- Maximum per year:
  - Adults: **CHF 700**
  - Children: **CHF 350**

### Maternity Exception

For maternity-related costs:
- **No franchise** applies
- **No Selbstbehalt** applies
- Full coverage from first franc

## Premium Structure

KVG premiums are determined by several factors:

### Premium Factors

| Factor | Description | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Premium Region** | BAG-defined zones (1-3 per canton) | Higher in urban areas |
| **Age Group** | Child, Young Adult, Adult | Adults pay most |
| **Franchise** | Higher franchise = lower premium | Up to 25% savings |
| **Accident Inclusion** | With/without UVG accident coverage | ~7% difference |
| **Insurance Model** | Standard, HMO, Hausarzt, Telmed | Up to 25% savings |

### What Does NOT Affect KVG Premiums

| Factor | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **Gender** | üìã Unisex premiums mandatory (Art. 61 KVG, since 2013) |
| **Health status** | No risk selection allowed |
| **Pre-existing conditions** | Cannot be excluded |
| **Claims history** | No bonus/malus system |

KVG allows alternative insurance models with premium discounts:

## Changing Insurers

### Ordinary Termination

| Deadline | Effective Date | Requirements |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| **November 30** | January 1 | Written notice to current insurer |

### Extraordinary Termination

Allowed mid-year in these cases:
- **Premium increase**: Special termination right (by end of month after notification)
- **Move**: To different premium region
- **VVG cancellation**: When linked to KVG product

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important**: New coverage must be confirmed before KVG can be terminated. Gaps in coverage are not allowed.

### Termination Letter Requirements

- Written form (letter or defined electronic methods)
- Must be **received** by insurer by deadline (not just sent)
- Must specify termination date (end of calendar year/month)

## Special Situations

### Accident Coverage (UVG)

- Employees are covered by employer's accident insurance (UVG)
- They can **exclude** accident from KVG (lower premium)
- Self-employed/unemployed: accident must be **included** in KVG

```
Employee (> 8h/week):     KVG WITHOUT accident  (cheaper)
                          UVG covers accidents

Self-employed/Unemployed: KVG WITH accident     (more expensive)
                          No UVG coverage
```

### Premium Subsidies (Pr√§mienverbilligung)

- Low-income residents can receive cantonal subsidies
- Amount varies by canton
- Apply through cantonal authority, not insurer

üìã **Info**: [Premium subsidies by canton](https://www.bag.admin.ch/bag/de/home/versicherungen/krankenversicherung/krankenversicherung-versicherte-mit-wohnsitz-in-der-schweiz/praemienverbilligung.html)

### Children and Family Discounts

- Children (0-18): Lower premiums, often no franchise
- Third+ child: Additional discounts in some cantons
- Young adults (19-25): Reduced rates
