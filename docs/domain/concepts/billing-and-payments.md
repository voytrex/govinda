# Billing and Payments

## Overview

Swiss health insurance uses various billing frequencies and payment methods. Understanding these options helps policyholders manage their insurance costs effectively, as some frequencies offer premium discounts.

## Billing Frequencies

### Available Options

| Frequency | Invoices/Year | Typical Discount | Best For |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|----------|
| **Monthly** | 12 | 0% | Cash flow management |
| **Quarterly** | 4 | 0.5% | Balance of flexibility and savings |

| **Annual** | 1 | 2% | Maximum savings |

Electronic billing through Swiss e-banking:

1. Policyholder activates eBill for insurer in e-banking
2. Invoices appear directly in e-banking
3. Can set up automatic payment or approve each invoice
4. Paperless - environmentally friendly

### KVG Protection

âš ï¸ **Important**: Basic insurance (KVG) coverage **cannot be suspended** for non-payment. The insurer must continue to cover mandatory benefits.

However:
- Debt collection proceedings will be initiated
- Interest and fees accumulate
- Person may be placed on cantonal "black list" (Schwarze Liste)
- Some cantons: only emergency coverage until debt is paid

### Black List (Schwarze Liste)

Some cantons maintain lists of persons with unpaid health insurance premiums:
- Coverage limited to emergency treatment
- Cannot change insurers until debt is cleared
- Controversial policy (not all cantons use it)
