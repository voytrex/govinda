# Franchise System (Annual Deductible)

## Overview

The **Franchise** (Jahresfranchise) is the annual deductible in Swiss basic health insurance (KVG). It is the amount you pay out of pocket before the insurance begins covering costs.

> **German**: Franchise, Jahresfranchise
> **French**: Franchise annuelle
> **Italian**: Franchigia annuale

## Available Franchise Levels

### Children (0-18 years)

| Franchise | Monthly Premium Impact | Best For |
|-----------|----------------------|----------|
| **CHF 0** | Highest premium | Frequent medical needs |
| CHF 100 | Slightly lower | Regular care needed |
| CHF 200 | Lower | Moderate care |
| CHF 300 | Lower | Occasional care |
| CHF 400 | Lower | Healthy child |
| **CHF 600** | Lowest premium | Very healthy child |

### Adults (19+ years)

| Franchise | Monthly Premium Impact | Typical Savings | Best For |
|-----------|----------------------|-----------------|----------|
| **CHF 300** | Highest (reference) | 0% | Frequent medical needs |
| CHF 500 | ~8% lower | ~CHF 20-40/month | Regular care |
| CHF 1000 | ~15% lower | ~CHF 40-70/month | Occasional care |
| CHF 1500 | ~20% lower | ~CHF 60-100/month | Mostly healthy |
| CHF 2000 | ~23% lower | ~CHF 80-120/month | Very healthy |
| **CHF 2500** | ~25% lower | ~CHF 100-150/month | Rarely needs care |

‚ö†Ô∏è **Legal Minimum**: Adults must have at least CHF 300 franchise.

## Choosing Your Franchise

### The Break-Even Calculation

Compare total costs (premium + out-of-pocket) at different health scenarios:

```
Example: Adult in Z√ºrich, 2024

Scenario A: CHF 300 franchise
- Monthly premium: CHF 400
- Annual premium: CHF 4,800

Scenario B: CHF 2,500 franchise
- Monthly premium: CHF 300
- Annual premium: CHF 3,600

Premium savings with B: CHF 1,200/year

BREAK-EVEN: If annual medical costs > CHF 1,200,
            the CHF 300 franchise is better.

If medical costs = CHF 0:
  - Option A total: CHF 4,800
  - Option B total: CHF 3,600  ‚úì Saves CHF 1,200

If medical costs = CHF 3,000:
  - Option A: CHF 4,800 + CHF 300 + CHF 270 = CHF 5,370
  - Option B: CHF 3,600 + CHF 2,500 + CHF 50 = CHF 6,150
  - Option A saves CHF 780  ‚úì
```

### Decision Framework

| Your Health Profile | Recommended Franchise |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| Chronic condition, regular medications | CHF 300 |
| Planning pregnancy | CHF 300 (but maternity is exempt) |
| Average health, occasional visits | CHF 500 - 1,000 |
| Very healthy, rarely sees doctor | CHF 1,500 - 2,500 |
| Young, healthy, emergency only | CHF 2,500 |

### Risk Considerations

| Franchise | Risk Level | Emergency Fund Needed |
|-----------|------------|----------------------|
| CHF 300 | Low | CHF 1,000 |
| CHF 500 | Low | CHF 1,200 |
| CHF 1,000 | Medium | CHF 1,700 |
| CHF 1,500 | Medium-High | CHF 2,200 |
| CHF 2,000 | High | CHF 2,700 |
| CHF 2,500 | Highest | CHF 3,200 |

‚ö†Ô∏è **Tip**: Keep an emergency fund of franchise + max Selbstbehalt (CHF 700).

## Franchise Rules

### Annual Reset

- Franchise resets every **January 1st**
- Previous year's payments don't carry over
- Full franchise applies again from first medical cost

### Changing Your Franchise

| When | Requirement | Deadline |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **January 1 only** | Written notice to insurer | **November 30** |
| Mid-year | Not allowed | N/A |

### Per-Person Tracking

- Each insured person has their own franchise
- Family members tracked separately
- Children's franchise separate from parents'

## Franchise + Selbstbehalt Combined

### Maximum Annual Out-of-Pocket

| Category | Franchise | + Selbstbehalt | = Max Total |
|----------|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| **Child (CHF 0)** | CHF 0 | CHF 350 | **CHF 350** |
| **Child (CHF 600)** | CHF 600 | CHF 350 | **CHF 950** |
| **Adult (CHF 300)** | CHF 300 | CHF 700 | **CHF 1,000** |
| **Adult (CHF 2,500)** | CHF 2,500 | CHF 700 | **CHF 3,200** |

### Calculation Example

```
Adult with CHF 1,000 franchise
Annual medical costs: CHF 5,000

Step 1: Franchise
  You pay: CHF 1,000
  Remaining: CHF 4,000

Step 2: Selbstbehalt (10%)
  You pay: CHF 400 (10% of CHF 4,000)
  Insurance pays: CHF 3,600

Step 3: Total
  Your out-of-pocket: CHF 1,000 + CHF 400 = CHF 1,400
  Insurance paid: CHF 3,600

If costs were CHF 15,000:
  Franchise: CHF 1,000
  Selbstbehalt: CHF 700 (capped, not 10% of CHF 14,000)
  Your total: CHF 1,700
  Insurance pays: CHF 13,300
```

## Special Cases

### Maternity (Mutterschaft)

üìã **Art. 64 Abs. 7 KVG**: No cost sharing for maternity benefits.

| Benefit | Franchise | Selbstbehalt |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| Prenatal care | ‚ùå Exempt | ‚ùå Exempt |
| Childbirth | ‚ùå Exempt | ‚ùå Exempt |
| Postnatal care (8 weeks) | ‚ùå Exempt | ‚ùå Exempt |

### Accident Coverage

If accident is included in KVG:
- Same franchise applies to accidents
- Selbstbehalt rules apply

If accident excluded (employee with UVG):
- KVG franchise doesn't apply to accidents
- UVG has its own rules

### Prevention Programs

Some BAG-approved prevention measures have:
- Reduced or no cost sharing
- Check specific program rules
